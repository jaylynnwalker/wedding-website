Travel

You will need a passport, an Indian e-visa, and your plane tickets. For more details on the travel documents, click here.

We suggest you plan for a 7 - 10 day trip. Click here to view an example itinerary. Sunny & Jaylynn are organizing two days of group sightseeing in Jodhpur. We also have contacts for a tour guide if you are interested in sightseeing in other cities. Just hit us up!

You may choose to book tickets through your preferred travel agent or book flights yourself. The Patel's family travel agent is DJ. You can contact him at 832-613-7028. If you would like to book your flights yourself, here are some flight suggestions to get you started.

If you want someone to double check your tickets before you buy them, feel free to reach out to us!

Hot-tip: Purchasing tickets through a travel agent restricts the airline's access to your ticket information. We recommend you book your tickets through the airline in case changes need to be made during your travel.

Expect to pay $2,000 - $3,000 for an economy class plane ticket from Houston to India.

We recommend buying your plane tickets at least 4 - 6 months in advance.

Hotel accomodations will be arranged by us for the nights of the wedding. The cost of your room will be covered for the nights of January 4, 5, and 6.

If you will be staying additional nights before or after the wedding, you will be responsible for the cost of your room for the additional nights. Contact us if you would like to book additional nights in the same room you will be staying in for the wedding.

General

If you are in the Houston area, there are plenty of Indian stores in the Hillcroft area. For a list of stores, click here. We recommend Sari Sansar for men and women's clothing.

If you would like to purchase clothing online, here are some guides that give information about traditional Indian attire. For women's clothing, click here and for men's wear click here.

There are many languages in India! The languages vary by state and the specific dialects vary within each state. Hindi is the official language of India and will be understood by a large portion of people. If you would like to learn some Hindi, here's a video of basic phrases!

The average low temperatures in Jodhpur in January is 51°F and the average high is 75°F. Expect chilly mornings and nights and warm afternoons.

All wedding events will be outdoors, so please plan to wear warm clothes for the evening events.
